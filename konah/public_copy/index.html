Vision and Mission

Our mission is to inspire and nurture the leaders of tomorrow. We are committed to providing an inclusive and stimulating learning environment that promotes academic excellence, personal growth, and a lifelong love of learning.

Academic Excellence

Our academic programs are designed to challenge and engage students, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills. We offer a broad and balanced curriculum that includes core subjects, humanities, languages, creative arts, technology, and physical education.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in the holistic development of our students. Our extensive extracurricular program includes sports teams, clubs and societies, performing arts, and community service.

Facilities

SFS Higher Secondary School Daili Village boasts state-of-the-art facilities to support our diverse programs including modern classrooms, science laboratories, a comprehensive library, art studios, music rooms, and athletic facilities.

Admissions

We invite prospective families to learn more about our vibrant community. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent and inclusive.

Learn More about Admissions

Parent and Community Engagement

At SFS Higher Secondary School Daili Village, we value strong partnerships with parents and the community. We encourage active parent involvement through PTAs, regular communication, volunteer opportunities, and community events.
