About Us

SFS School, Daili Village, Kangpokpi, is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in a nurturing environment. Guided by the values of St. Francis de Sales, we aim to develop well-rounded individuals with strong moral character, leadership skills, and a commitment to community service. Our holistic approach ensures that students excel both academically and personally, preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
